Coverage: Both Breasts. Scan time will be 22 min

- May up FOV if needed. DO NOT DECREASE FOV.

- May change slice thickness if needed. DO NOT CHANGE NUMBER OF LOCS PER SLAB

Scanning Notes: Scan runs in 2 parts. First part is ~12.5 minutes. Second part is ~ 9 minutes.

Coach patient to hold very still through both parts.

First part: Autoprescan. Scan. No need to check values. Scanner will pause when first part is done.

Second part: Tell patient to expect contrast, but keep still. Inject and hit scan simultaneously. No injection delay, 2 ml/sec, 0.1 mmol/kg Gd, 20 ml Flush.

Expect ~ 8 minutes after scan is done to finish recon

Post scan: Highlight WATER:DISCO 4D series in Browser. Open c-shell. Type "msdt2" command. Hit "confirm" in the window that pops up. This will create pre and peak contrast series. Use peak contrast series to make Sagittal reformats, Axial and Sagittal TS MIPS and rotating MIPS.
